About this page

The Sustainability Auditing Tool is the brainchild of the Orthogonal Research and Education Lab, whose intent is to introduce collective cognition modeling to open-source software communities. If you're interested in modeling your community, all you need is a GitHub account to get started. Click the login button above to authenticate your GitHub account, and get modeling!

Support for Sustainability Auditing Tool

If your community is experiencing difficulties authenticating its GitHub account, populating models, or any other technical issue, please reach out to us at sustainabilityauditingtool@gmail.com
